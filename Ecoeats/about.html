Rescuing Good Food Before It Goes to Waste

EcoEats connects near-expiry groceries with smart shoppers who care about savings and sustainability.

Our Mission

Reduce avoidable food waste by making near-expiry inventory easy to discover and affordable for everyone.

Our Impact

Every rescued item means fewer landfill emissions and more value from food already produced.

For Stores
